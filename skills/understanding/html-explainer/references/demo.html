Explainer Review: how this page works

This is a demo explainer with the review annotator loaded. Hover over any paragraph, heading, list item, or table cell — it gets a dashed outline. Click it, type a comment, and it sticks (saved in your browser, survives reloads).

To comment on a specific sentence inside a long paragraph, select that text first, then click — the selection becomes the quote in the export instead of the paragraph's opening words.

The workflow

  • Click blocks and add comments — edit requests or questions, both work.
  • Add as many as you want, across as many blocks as you want.
  • Hit Copy feedback (bottom-right) and paste the block into your AI chat.
  • The agent locates each reference, applies edits to the file, answers questions in chat, and summarizes what it did.
